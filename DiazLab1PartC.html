Fluffy Buttermilk Pancakes

List of Ingredients
  • Butter
  • Buttermilk
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Backing Powder
  • Sugar
  • Egg
  • Salt
  • Flour

  1. Sift 1 and 1/3 cups of flour, 2 tbsp of sugar, 1 tsp of baking powder, and 1 tsp of salt into a large bowl and mix till combined.
  2. In another bowl, add 1 cup of buttermilk, 1 egg, 2 tsp of vanilla extract, and 4 tbsp of melted butter and mix till combined
  3. Mix wet and dry ingredients until barely combined ensuring no clumps of flour (do not overmix as the gluten will activate and make pancakes less fluffy.
  4. Heat a pan over medium heat with some butter and add about 1/2 cup of batter to pan.
  5. Cook until golden brown and cooked trhough.
